Fortnite

Fortnite is an online video game and game platform developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is available in six distinct game mode versions that otherwise share the same general gameplay and game engine: Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play battle royale game in which up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing; Fortnite: Save the World, a cooperative hybrid tower defense-shooter and survival game in which up to four players fight off zombie-like creatures and defend objects with traps and fortifications they can build; and Fortnite Creative, in which players are given complete freedom to create worlds and battle arenas, Lego Fortnite, an open world survival game, Rocket Racing, a racing game, and Fortnite Festival, a rhythm game.

Counter-Strike 2

Counter-Strike 2 (abbreviated: CS2), formerly also Counter-Strike: Source 2(CS:S2), is a competitive multiplayer first -person shooter(FPS) game developed by Valve Corporation on their Source 2 game engine. The game is part of the Counter-Strike series, a computer game series of similar first-person shooters, and is the fifth game in the main series. Valve announced the game on March 22, 2023 and released it as an open beta on September 1 with a full release on September 27 of the same year. The game is a sequel, free upgrade and replacement of Counter-Strike: Global Offensive(CS:GO), promising more stable underlying system, new tick system, new competitive features and details, renovated courses and game balance, better graphics and more.

GTA

Grand Theft Auto (usually abbreviated GTA , American English for: "gross appropriation of means of transportation") is an award-winning action game series about the modern gangster life, where guns, money, vehicles and sex play a big role. The games are developed by Rockstar North (formerly DMA Design), Rockstar Leeds, Rockstar Toronto and Rockstar Lincoln. All games in the series are published by Rockstar Games. It was intended during the development phase that the first game would be called Race'n'Chase, and was close to being canceled due to production problems.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a 2011 sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios and originally released in 2009. The game was created by Markus "Notch" Persson in the Java programming language. Following several early private testing versions, it was first made public in May 2009 before being fully released on November 18, 2011, with Notch stepping down and Jens "Jeb" Bergensten taking over development. Minecraft has become the best-selling video game in history, with over 300 million copies sold and nearly 140 million monthly active players as of 2023. Over the years following its release, it has been ported to several platforms, including PC, mobile and various consoles.

De tio bästsäljande spelen genom tiderna har alla gemensamt att de erbjuder enastående spelupplevelser som har fångat spelarnas intresse över hela världen. Dessa spel utmärker sig genom att kombinera engagerande spelmekanik, innovativa designkoncept och hög kvalitet. De har revolutionerat sina respektive genrer, från öppna världar och kreativa sandlådor till intensiva multiplayer-upplevelser och beroendeframkallande pusselspel. Sammanfattningsvis, de tio bästsäljande spelen har satt standarden för vad som gör ett spel minnesvärt och framgångsrikt, och de fortsätter att inspirera både spelare och utvecklare världen över.
